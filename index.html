About Dr Hungwe

Dr. Taurai Hungwe, PhD (SMU), MIT (UP), M.Ed. (Wits), BSc Hons (UZ), epitomizes excellence in the realm of Information Technology education and professional development. As a distinguished figure in his field, Dr. Hungwe's unwavering dedication to fostering brilliance among aspiring IT professionals is evident through his multifaceted roles. With his credentials as a MICT SETA Registered Assessor and Moderator, Dr. Hungwe plays a pivotal role in shaping the trajectory of the IT industry by guiding individuals through a spectrum of qualifications. His expertise spans National Certificates in Systems Support, Systems Development, and End User Computing, as well as Further Education and Training Certificates in Systems Development and Technical Support. Through meticulous assessment and moderation processes, Dr. Hungwe ensures that individuals under his mentorship receive comprehensive education and training, equipping them with the skills for thriving careers in Information Technology. Beyond his pivotal role with MICT SETA, Dr. Hungwe holds a prestigious position as a university lecturer at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University. Within the Computer Science and Information Technology Department, he imparts dynamic teaching methodologies enriched by his practical experience, inspiring students to excel in their academic pursuits. Additionally, Dr. Hungwe actively supervises Honours and Masters students, nurturing a culture of innovation and research excellence within the academic community.
